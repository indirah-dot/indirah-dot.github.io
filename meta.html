The Path of Least Resistance

Most websites are bloated with tracking, scripts, and noise. For this archive, I wanted the "absolute floor"—raw content, zero scripts, and maximum speed.

1. The Challenge

I pushed the AI to stop overcomplicating the code. I wanted "Simple. 10 lines or less."

The pivot toward extreme minimalism.

2. Trimming the Fat

We eventually stripped it down even further. No styling, no headers—just the raw signal. This is the "Sovereign" philosophy: pure power with no bloat.

"The absolute floor." A valid website in 5 lines.

3. Mobile Infrastructure

To keep the project accessible, we used the GitHub App for iPhone. This allowed me to "Direct Cloud Edit" the site while standing in line for coffee.

Setting up the "One-Handed" deployment.
